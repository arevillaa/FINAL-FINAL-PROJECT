ABOUT NOD-KRAI

Nod-Krai is a newly accessed Cryo-based region bordering Snezhnaya, focused heavily on the forgotten lore of Teyvat’s Three Moons. It introduces Kuuvahki, a mysterious ancient power, along with new regional factions like the Frostmoon Scions and Fatui Harbingers seeking control of its frozen secrets.

Kuuvahki Ancient Power

WHAT IS KUUVAHKI?

Kuuvahki is an ancient power tied to the Three Moons, granting Travelers new exploration abilities. Its use is contested by mystical factions— and aggressively pursued by the Fatui.

Nod-Krai’s Ruler

NO ARCHON

Nod-Krai is autonomous; no Archon rules it. Its conflicts center on forgotten Moon Goddess figures and pre-elemental history older than the Seven.

Current Limited Banner

NEFFER — CRYO DPS

Neffer freezes enemies with her skill, then releases a massive Cryo projectile during her Burst for devastating single-target damage.
