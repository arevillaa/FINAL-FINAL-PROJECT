About Nod-Krai

Nod-Krai is a mysterious frozen land located south of Snezhnaya. Its people are strong, its lands unforgiving, and its loyalties uncertain. Ruled by unseen powers and fiercely defended, it is said that even the Fatui fear what sleeps beneath its ice.

KUUVANKI Ancient Power

Kuuvahki

Kuuvahki is an ancient geothermal force buried beneath Nod-Krai’s crust. Its energy is feared, harnessed, and exploited — and aggressively pursued by the Fatui.
